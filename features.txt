Gamebot Features:

 - Shows new likes on your maps/mods between your last and latest command
 - Highlights featured maps with yellow color
 - You can now check your assets list
 - More details to profile command, such as number of maps/assets/mods, partnership etc
 - You can count total maps plays on profiles
 - You can check how many plays are online on a map
 - You can check the maps added to a player's favourites
 - Seperate command for viewing a single map in details, shows thumb, description, likes, plays, version and lot more
 - You can start tracking likes and plays and view them in the form of graph
